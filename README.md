# Inventory-Management-Analysis:
Inventory Management Analysis project delivering actionable insights on inventory performance, sales trends, warehouse efficiency, order distribution, and revenue forecasting through interactive Power BI and Excel dashboards.

# Key Insights & Features:
1.Revenue and cost analysis by year and category
2.Warehouse performance based on revenue and quantity sold
3.Inventory and sales trends across product categories
4.Actual vs forecast revenue for demand planning
5.Country-wise order distribution and shipment analysis
6.Interactive filters for Category and Year

# Feedback & Collaboration:
Collaborated with stakeholders to define KPIs and ensure data accuracy. Delivered an interactive dashboard supporting inventory planning and business decision-making.
